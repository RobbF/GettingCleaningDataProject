##Code Book

Course Project for Getting and Cleaning Data

============================================

subject  
Integers 1 through 30 identifying the experiment's 30 volunteers (age 19-48)

activity  
The six activities observed for each of the thirty subjects:  
1. walking  
2. walking upstairs  
3. walking downstairs  
4. sitting  
5. standing  
6. laying
